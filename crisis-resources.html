Immediate Steps You Can Take

1

Reach out to someone you trust. Call a friend, family member, or neighbor and tell them how you're feeling.

2

Remove any dangerous items. If possible, move away from things that could be used to hurt yourself.

3

Go to the nearest Emergency Room. If you cannot stay safe, hospital staff can provide a secure environment and immediate care.
