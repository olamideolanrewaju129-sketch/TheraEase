Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, book a session, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including to process transactions, send you technical notices, and communicate with you about products, services, offers, and events.

3. Data Security

We implement specific security measures to assist in protecting your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage of information can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to help us understand user behavior, administer the website, serve advertisements, and populate search results.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@theraease.com.
