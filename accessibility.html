Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Commitment to Accessibility

TheraEase is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. We are continuously working to improve the user experience for all users and to apply the relevant accessibility standards.

2. Actions Taken

We take the following measures to ensure accessibility of TheraEase:

  • Include accessibility as part of our internal policies.
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.

3. Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. TheraEase is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA.

4. Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of TheraEase. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers by contacting us at accessibility@theraease.com.

5. Technical Specifications

Accessibility of TheraEase relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
